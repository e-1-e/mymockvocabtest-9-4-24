GRADING PROCESS:
for each word/root stem, your input goes through several changes:
  - first it is converted to all lower case (so that way its not case sensitive)
  - then it gets stripped of all leading and trailing whitespace (so that way if ur phone decides to add a space at the end of every word ur not penalized for that. just dont add spaces in the middle of the word.
  - next it is compared to the word to the left of its box
  - u are given a point if they match exactly
